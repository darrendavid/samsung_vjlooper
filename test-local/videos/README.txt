Place your test video files in this directory.

Supported formats:
- MP4 (.mp4, .m4v)
- MKV (.mkv)
- AVI (.avi)
- MOV (.mov)
- WMV (.wmv)
- FLV (.flv)
- WebM (.webm)

The local server will automatically detect and serve any video files placed here.

For quick testing, you can:
1. Use short videos (10-30 seconds) for faster testing
2. Use different formats to test compatibility
3. Add at least 2-3 videos to test the random selection and crossfade

Example files you could use:
- sample1.mp4
- sample2.mp4
- sample3.mp4
